Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I park the car?

Our cars have a Z permit permit, which allows you to park the car in any A-O resident parking space in Helsinki. Elsewhere in the capital area, the car can be parked in unrestricted public parking spots.

The car can only be picked up and returned in our suggested parking spaces, but driving and parking the car outside capital region is possible during the rental. The possible parking costs are covered by the renter.

What if the car stops working?

Just phone (09 7258 4400) at any time when there is something wrong with the car. By cooperating with Autoliitto, we provide our customers 24/7 support service.

Do I need to fill up the tank? What if I fill it up more than I will consume for the trip?

You don’t need to fill up the tank when you return the car, as long as the fuel warning light is not on. The fuel fee is included in the final price.

When needed, we will provide you with a fuel code through the app.

How do I pay for the service?

You can pay with your internet bank account or credit card. We will send the invoice at the end of the service.

What if small bumps/scratches happens?

We have open and flexible policy about minor damages to the car. We won’t charge you anything if the small bumps or scratches won’t affect the functionality of the car.
